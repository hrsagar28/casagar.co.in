Weekly Updates

For the Week of August 18, 2025

  • Economic Outlook: S&P has upgraded India's sovereign credit rating, citing strong growth. The stock market reacted positively, with the Sensex and Nifty surging.
  • GST: The government is moving forward with "GST 2.0," which aims to rationalize the rate structure, likely to two main slabs.
  • Income Tax: The new Income Tax Bill, 2025, has been passed. Key changes include a higher tax rebate limit and an increased standard deduction.
  • Company Law & Auditing: The MCA has extended the deadline for the dematerialisation of shares for private companies. New rules will mandate an audit trail feature in accounting software.
