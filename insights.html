Week of August 18, 2025

Economic Outlook

S&P has upgraded India's sovereign credit rating, citing strong growth prospects and improved fiscal management. The stock market reacted positively, with the Sensex and Nifty surging to new highs.

GST Developments

The government is moving forward with "GST 2.0," which aims to rationalize the rate structure to two main slabs, potentially simplifying compliance and boosting business confidence.

Income Tax Reform

The new Income Tax Bill, 2025, has been passed. Key changes include a higher tax rebate limit under Section 87A and an increased standard deduction for salaried employees.

Corporate Compliance

The MCA has extended the deadline for dematerialisation of shares for private companies. New audit trail requirements for accounting software will be mandatory from April 2026.
