Corporate Compliance Made Simple

Maintaining compliance with the Companies Act and Registrar of Companies (ROC) is essential for every registered company. We provide comprehensive secretarial services to manage these obligations efficiently, preventing penalties and ensuring your company maintains a good legal standing.

Services Included:

  • Annual ROC Filings

    Preparation and filing of annual returns and financial statements (Forms AOC-4, MGT-7) with the ROC.

  • Maintenance of Statutory Registers

    Keeping all mandatory statutory records and registers updated as required by law.

  • Event-Based Compliances

    Managing filings related to changes in directorship, share capital, registered office, or amendments to the company's charter documents.

  • Secretarial Advisory

    Providing expert advice on matters of corporate governance, board meetings, and shareholder communications.
