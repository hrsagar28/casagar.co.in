Our Audit Approach

Our audit and assurance services are designed to provide stakeholders with confidence in your financial reporting. We conduct our audits with a commitment to objectivity, integrity, and professional skepticism, identifying risks and opportunities for improving your internal controls and business processes.

Services Included:

  • Statutory Audit

    Comprehensive audits as required under the Companies Act, 2013, ensuring full compliance with regulatory standards.

  • Tax Audit

    Detailed audits under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act, 1961, to verify the accuracy of your tax-related records.

  • Internal & Management Audits

    In-depth reviews of your internal controls and operational efficiency to identify areas for improvement and risk mitigation.

  • Certification Services

    Providing various certifications required for regulatory purposes, including net worth certificates and 15CB forms.
