Checklist for Salaried Individuals

For FY 2024-25 (AY 2025-26). Covers salary and rental income.

Print

Basic Information

  • Your Income Tax Login ID and Password.
  • Copy of your PAN Card and Aadhaar Card.
  • Your primary Mobile Number and Email ID.
  • List of all operative bank accounts with IFSC codes.

Income & Deductions

  • Form 16: From all employers if you changed jobs.
  • Salary Arrears: If received, please provide Form 10E [?] A form required to claim tax relief on salary received in arrears or advance. .
  • Bank Statements: For all savings and salary accounts.
  • Other Income: Details of interest on savings/FDs.
  • Foreign Income/Assets: Details of any foreign assets or income.

  • Proofs for Tax-Saving Deductions (Old Regime):

    • 80C: Proofs for LIC, PPF, ELSS, NSC, Tuition Fees, etc.
    • Home Loan: Annual interest certificate from your bank.
    • HRA [?] House Rent Allowance. Provide rent receipts to claim exemption. Landlord's PAN is needed if annual rent exceeds â‚¹1,00,000. : Rent receipts.
    • 80D: Health insurance premium receipts.
    • 80G: Donation receipts (with institution's PAN).
    • 80E: Statement for interest paid on an education loan.

Income from House Property

  • Rental Agreements for all rented-out properties.
  • Municipal Tax Receipts.
  • Co-owner's Details (Name, PAN, and ownership share %).
  • Home Loan Certificate for the rented property.

Have other types of income?

For sale of property or shares, see: Checklist for Capital Gains.
For business or freelance income, see: Checklist for Business (Presumptive).
